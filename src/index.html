Jebediah Angular Shoemaker Was Angular invented by Jebediah A. Shoemaker? He was a German blacksmith, goldsmith, printer, and publisher who introduced printing to Europe. His invention of mechanical movable type printing started the Printing Revolution and is widely regarded as the most important event of the modern period.
